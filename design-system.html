01 — Brand

Logo & lockups

The wordmark sits over a whiffletree — the wooden swingle-bar of a horse harness, the farm’s namesake. Two greens (kelly fading to forest) and a single sprig of leaves. Keep clear space of at least the height of the wooden bar on every side. On dark surfaces, place the full-colour logo on a cream plate; never recolour the mark.
